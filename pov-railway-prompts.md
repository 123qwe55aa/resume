# 🎬 POV 铁轨梦境 — 运镜脚本 / Prompt 文件

## V1（8-10s，单轨直推）

**整体设定：** 第一人称 POV，镜头始终贴近铁轨中心向前推进。
风格：梦幻油画，厚涂笔触，金粉色夕阳 → 蓝色海底世界。

---

### 锁定句（可附加到每段末尾）

> same continuous first-person railway journey, rails always centered from bottom foreground into the distance, consistent dreamlike oil painting style, cinematic depth, smooth forward camera motion, no cuts inside the shot

---

### 分段 Prompts

#### 0-2s — 花海云端开场
```
POV camera moves forward smoothly along centered railway tracks through a glowing pink flower field above the clouds. Petals drift toward the lens, golden sunset ahead, distant fantasy mountains, romantic oil painting style, gentle acceleration, rails remain stable and centered, no text, no watermark.
[锁定句]
```

#### 2-4s — 花海右转
```
POV camera continues forward along the same railway tracks and banks slightly right with the curve. Foreground pink flowers and petals rush past with painterly motion blur, speed increases, golden sunset and cloud sea remain visible, immersive roller-coaster POV, consistent oil painting style, no text, no watermark.
[锁定句]
```

#### 4-6s — 花海→海底过渡
```
POV camera rushes forward and slightly downward along the same centered railway tracks. The glowing pink flower field gradually transforms into a luminous underwater ocean world; petals turn into bubbles, clouds dissolve into blue water, golden sunset shifts into blue sun rays, and jellyfish begin to appear. The railway remains continuous and unbroken, magical seamless transition, oil painting style, no text, no watermark.
[锁定句]
```

#### 6-8s — 海底鲸鱼水母
```
POV camera moves forward smoothly along glowing railway tracks underwater. A graceful whale swims beside the rails on the right, translucent jellyfish drift around the camera, soft blue sun rays come from above, bubbles and particles pass the lens, speed slows into a dreamy glide, consistent oil painting style, no text, no watermark.
[锁定句]
```

#### 8-10s — 云海/海底收尾（可选）
```
POV camera continues gliding along the underwater railway into the distance, slightly tilting upward toward bright blue light. The whale slowly passes by, jellyfish float around the rails, bubbles shimmer in the water, calm majestic ending, oil painting texture, cinematic 16:9, no text, no watermark.
[锁定句]
```

## V2（8-10s，S 弯盘旋运镜）

**整体设定：** 第一人称 POV，铁轨走 S 弯 + 盘旋，过山车式动态。
风格：梦幻油画，厚涂笔触，金粉色夕阳 → 蓝色海底世界。

---

### 锁定句

> continuous first-person POV railway journey, the rails stay visually connected and centered from the bottom foreground, the track twists left and right in elegant S-curves, dynamic banking motion, immersive roller-coaster feeling, consistent dreamlike oil painting style, no text, no watermark, no people

---

### 分段 Prompts

#### 0-2s — 花海开场
```
POV camera glides forward along glowing railway tracks through a pink flower field above a sea of clouds. The rails begin at the bottom center, then gently weave left and right in a wide S-curve toward distant fantasy mountains. Petals fly past the lens, golden sunset ahead, warm rose-orange light, cinematic depth, oil painting texture, thick impasto brush strokes, gentle acceleration.
[锁定句]
```

#### 2-4s — 花海盘旋加速
```
POV camera accelerates along the same railway tracks as they curve sharply right, then sweep back left around a cliff above the clouds. The camera banks with the rails like a roller coaster, foreground flowers stretch into painterly motion blur, petals rush toward the viewer, dramatic sunset flare, strong sense of speed, romantic fantasy oil painting.
[锁定句]
```

#### 4-6s — 花海穿越进海底
```
POV camera dives forward and downward along the same railway tracks as they spiral in a smooth descending curve. The pink flower field and golden cloud sea gradually transform into a luminous underwater ocean world; petals turn into bubbles, clouds dissolve into blue water, sunlight becomes blue ocean rays, jellyfish begin appearing around the rails. The railway remains continuous and unbroken, magical seamless transition, dynamic banking motion, oil painting style.
[锁定句]
```

#### 6-8s — 海底鲸鱼水母
```
POV camera continues underwater along glowing railway tracks that weave left and right between floating jellyfish. A graceful whale swims beside the track, first appearing on the right, then gliding across the mid-distance as the rails curve left. Soft blue sun rays from above, bubbles and particles pass the lens, coral and sea plants near the lower edges, smooth roller-coaster motion slowed into a dreamy glide, richly textured oil painting.
[锁定句]
```

#### 8-10s — 可选收尾
```
POV camera follows the underwater railway into a wide elegant spiral, then the track straightens toward a bright blue light in the distance. The whale slowly passes overhead, jellyfish drift around the rails, bubbles shimmer through the water, motion becomes calmer and majestic, cinematic ending, consistent oil painting texture, centered rails, dreamlike ocean atmosphere.
[锁定句]
```

---

### 剪辑建议

| 时段 | 运镜 | 情绪 |
|------|------|------|
| 0-2s | 轻微 S 弯 | 建立世界 |
| 2-4s | 右弯再左弯，速度最快 🔥 | 加速高潮 |
| 4-6s | 下潜盘旋 | 花海→海底转场 |
| 6-8s | 海底左右蜿蜒 | 鲸鱼水母陪伴 |
| 8-10s | 盘旋后拉直 | 收尾 |

      ↑            ↑

## V1 vs V2 区别

| | V1 | V2 |
|--|----|----|
| 运镜 | 单轨直推 | S 弯 + 盘旋 + 过山车 |
| 运动感 | 平滑前推 | 动态倾侧 + 加速感更强 |
| 锁定句 | rails always centered, smooth forward | S-curves, dynamic banking, roller-coaster |
| 适用 | 舒缓叙事风 | 节奏更快、视觉冲击更强 |
